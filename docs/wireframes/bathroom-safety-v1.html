Common Questions

How long does a shower conversion take?

Most barrier-free shower conversions are completed in 3-5 days. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your home.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We offer several financing options including 0% interest for qualified buyers. We also help identify applicable VA benefits, Medicaid waivers, and other assistance programs.

Will my bathroom still look nice?

Absolutely. We specialize in universal design — modifications that are both safe and beautiful. Our barrier-free showers use premium materials and modern design principles.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

That's what the free assessment is for. Our certified specialist will evaluate your home and recommend the right solution for your specific situation.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. Our team holds CAPS (Certified Aging-in-Place Specialist) certification from the National Association of Home Builders.
