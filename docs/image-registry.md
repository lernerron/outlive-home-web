# Asset Permission Registry

This document serves as the central database tracking the legal and licensing status of all images used across the Outlive Homes website.

## Status Definitions
- **CLEARED** — Legally usable, commercially licensed, or explicitly approved.
- **PENDING** — Awaiting clearance or replacement.
- **REPLACE** — No license, must be replaced before production launch.
- **REMOVED** — Asset is no longer used but may still exist in the repository.
